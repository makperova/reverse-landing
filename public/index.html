Our story

Built for people whose money moves like they do.

The re:verse team

For twenty years, our team built fintech products across payments, cards, currencies and cross-border money movement. Those products processed billions in volume and served millions of people.

We know the rails, the risks and the trust it takes to move money.

But re:verse started from something more personal.

We are the people we are building for: founders, builders, travelers and remote teams whose financial lives no longer fit inside one country, one currency or one app.

Income arrives in one place. Bills hit in another. Savings sit somewhere else. Stablecoins, cards, accounts and currencies become part of the same life.

The tools exist. The admin still sits with you.

re:verse is an AI money agent built by people who needed one.

Privacy & security

Privacy by design.

Nothing gets access by default — not even the AI model.

01
Permission-checked
Every request is checked against the permissions you set. The model cannot give itself more access or work around your rules.
02
Data stays inside
Sensitive data does not go to external LLMs. Your financial context stays inside our protected infrastructure, linked to an internal ID and reduced to the minimum before it reaches the AI layer. No raw card or personal details are passed into the model.
03
Built to standards
PCI DSS and GDPR principles from day one: encryption, access control, audit trail, permission revocation and data deletion on request.
